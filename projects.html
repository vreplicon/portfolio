Gear Closet

Simplify planning your next adventure by using Gear Closet. With this app you can store the information for all of your gear in one place and easily put together a list of all of the things you want to bring on your next big trip. Built using React, NodeJS, Express, and PostgreSQL.

Quilt Calc

This app allows you to get real-time calculations of needed fabric for different quilt patterns. The user can choose from different classic quilt patterns and then change parameters such as length, width, and border sizes. Built using React, NodeJS, Express, and PostgreSQL.
