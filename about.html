A little about me...

Being born and raised in Silicon Valley, I guess you could say that I was bred to be a developer. Before I was the age of two, I was on the computer playing with my friends Freddie the Fish and Putt-Putt. By the time I was in middle school I had made my first little programs with Scratch, and by the time I graduated high school I was using C and Arduino to program robots and magnetic levitating train models.

During college I tried to tie my love of programming with my other passion of astronomy. This led to me having a wide range of opportunities including getting the chance to work at both Apple and NASA. Having this background has allowed me to pick up many different tools in the trade. Not only do I have experience in data science focused languages (Python) but also in more software development focused languages (Java, Javascript).
